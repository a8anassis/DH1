MSc Digital Humanities

The Postgraduate Program in Digital Methods for the Humanities aims to provide university graduates with specialized postgraduate knowledge in the development and application of digital methods in the humanities. The purpose of the Program is to promote knowledge, develop an interdisciplinary approach and research and prepare for productive and research participation in the development of the interdisciplinary area that has begun to be established under the name "Digital Humanities". Graduates of the Program will be able to take on research, productive, documentation, editing, organizing and managing information resources and management roles in projects with intense use of digital media and techniques in the fields of humanities and cultural heritage.
